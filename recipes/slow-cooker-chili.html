Slow Cooker Chili

Description

The slow cooker is a great way to make chili. This beefy chili is packed with beans, veggies, and spice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ¾ cup diced onion
  • ¾ cup diced celery
  • ¾ cup diced green bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans tomato puree
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans with liquid
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans with liquid
  • ½ tablespoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon dried basil
  • ¾ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon hot pepper sauce

Steps

  1. Place the beef in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook until evenly brown
  3. Drain grease
  4. Place the beef in a slow cooker
  5. Mix in onion, celery, green bell pepper, garlic, tomato puree, kidney beans, and cannellini beans
  6. Season with chili powder, parsley, salt, basil, oregano, black pepper, and hot pepper sauce
  7. Cover, and cook 8 hours on Low
